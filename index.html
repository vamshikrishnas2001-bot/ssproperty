Frequently Asked Questions

A Khata Transfer is the process of updating the BBMP municipal property record from the seller's name to the buyer's name after a property sale. It typically takes 7–21 working days depending on document completeness. With our expert assistance, we help you complete it as quickly as possible.
e-Aasthi is the Government of Karnataka's digital property record management system. It maintains digital records of all properties, ownership, and tax details. Having your property correctly registered on e-Aasthi is essential for legal ownership, loans, and future transactions.
